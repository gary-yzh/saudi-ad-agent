# Noor Souq — Brand Manual (excerpt)

> Internal reference document for ad creative production.
> Generated for the Cowork agent demo. Not a real brand.

## 1. Brand essence
Noor Souq is a Riyadh-based marketplace for premium home goods, modest fashion,
and dates / specialty foods. The voice is **warm, confident, family-first,
modern-traditional**. Never frivolous, never bro-y, never aggressive.

## 2. Visual identity
- Primary palette: deep emerald `#0E5C4A`, sand `#E7D7B8`, gold accent `#C9A24A`.
- Secondary: charcoal `#1F1F1F`, off-white `#FAF7F0`.
- Typography: **Tajawal** (Arabic + Latin). No Comic Sans, no neon gradients.
- Logo lock-up always sits with ≥1× clear space around it.

## 3. Photography & video direction
- Daylight, natural shadows, real Saudi homes / souqs / desert vistas.
- Faces must show **expression, not just product**. Modesty defaults: long
  sleeves for women, no exposed shoulders, head-cover optional but the default
  for spokesperson roles is hijab.
- No alcohol, no pork, no gambling iconography, no unrelated religious symbols.
- During Ramadan campaigns, avoid eating/drinking shots between Fajr and
  Maghrib; use evening *iftar* scenes instead.

## 4. Copy & tone
- Bilingual ads: Arabic primary, English secondary. Arabic copy must read
  right-to-left and be reviewed by a native speaker before launch.
- Always greet with **"أهلاً"** ("Ahlan") not "Hi". Sign-off
  **"تسوّق الآن"** ("Shop now") on commerce-led creatives.
- Never use the words: *cheap, deal-of-a-lifetime, drunk, party hard,
  binge, sin, bacon, pork, alcohol, beer, wine, whisky, casino, bet, gamble*.
- Avoid superlatives that overpromise ("the world's best", "guaranteed").

## 5. Cultural & regulatory guardrails
- Saudi GAMR / advertising rules: no comparative claims naming competitors,
  no health claims without SFDA approval, no children under 13 in food /
  beverage ads.
- Respect prayer times — do not prompt urgency ("act in the next 10 minutes")
  during scheduled adhan windows in target geos.
- Respect Ramadan: hero creatives during the holy month should evoke
  *family, generosity, reflection*, not discount frenzy.

## 6. Performance hints (for the Eval node)
- Hooks under 8 words historically score the highest CTR on Snap / TikTok KSA.
- Vertical 9:16, ≤15s, captions burned-in for sound-off viewing.
- One product, one promise per creative — multi-SKU mosaics underperform.
